How XAUUSD math works here

• 1 pip = $0.10 price move → $1 move = 10 pips
• Contract size = 100 oz per 1.00 lot
• P&L = (Exit − Entry) × Lots × 100
• So 0.01 lot × $1 move × 100 = $1
• RRR shown as a ratio — 1:2 means you risk 1 to make 2
